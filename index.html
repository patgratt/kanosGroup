About Kanos Marketing

Kanos Group exists to tell compelling stories about people, businesses, and organizations so we can create more meaningful connections in the world. We help organizations discover, craft, and communicate their authentic voice through intentional strategy.

We are a boutique team of curious and driven people committed to high-quality work and personal, lasting client relationships. We offer complete marketing support from strategy to analytics, or project-specific support anywhere in your funnel.

The Founder's Story

My name is Will Papazahariou and I founded Kanos Group to build a company that emphasizes the "why" behind every decision. When curious people meet purposeful businesses, great stories are created.

I believe messaging, storytelling, and community are the core of strong brands. A brand is often a company's most powerful asset, and I am obsessed with the details that leave a lasting impression.

My Past Work

I generated over $3M in revenue by leading and scaling sales teams at a financial services company, while helping shape messaging, values, and digital strategy.

Later, as a strategy consultant in NYC, I worked on market, brand, and corporate strategy initiatives across Streaming, Financial Services, and Alcoholic Beverages for Fortune 500 clients.
